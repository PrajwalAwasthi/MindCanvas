Frequently Asked Questions


How do I access the books?

To access the books, simply click on the book cover image or the provided link under each book listing. The links will open the corresponding book files in a new tab or window.

What file formats are the books available in?

The books are available in various file formats such as PDF, WEBP, JPG, and PNG. Make sure you have the appropriate software installed on your device to open and view these file formats.

Can I download the books for offline reading?

Yes, you can download the books for offline reading. When you click on the book links, you will be prompted to save or open the file. Choose the "Save" option to download the book to your device.

Are the books free to access?

Yes, all the books listed on this website are available for free access and download.

How can I search for a specific book or topic?

You can use the search bar at the top of the webpage to search for a specific book or topic. Start typing the book title, author, or keyword, and relevant suggestions will appear in the dropdown menu. Click on the desired result or press the "Search" button to see the matching books.

How often is the book collection updated?

Our team regularly updates the book collection to provide the latest and most relevant resources. However, we do not have a fixed schedule for updates. Keep checking the website periodically for new additions.

Can I suggest new books to be added?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions for new books to enhance our collection. Please use the "Contact" page to submit your book recommendations or any other feedback.

How do I report a broken or incorrect link?

If you encounter a broken or incorrect link for any of the books, please let us know by using the "Contact" page and providing the specific book details and link. We'll promptly investigate and fix the issue.


If you have any other questions or concerns, please feel free to reach out to us through the "Contact" page.
